# Privacy & Data Governance Policy: SaturnLH

**Effective Date:** 2026-06-05  
**Version:** 2.0.0 (Global Standards)

This Privacy Policy delineates the data acquisition, processing, and protection protocols enforced by SaturnLH within the Luciano's Hub ecosystem.

### I. Data Acquisition Protocols
Luciano's Hub maintains a policy of data minimization. We acquire only data requisite for the maintenance of operational stability:
*   **Primary Identifiers:** Discord User UID, Global Name, and associated server roles.
*   **Interaction Telemetry:** Logs generated through command execution, ticket creation, and engagement with modular tools.

### II. Data Stewardship and Utilization
Data is processed for the singular purpose of service optimization and community governance. We explicitly renounce the commercialization or unauthorized dissemination of user data to third-party entities.

### III. Data Integrity and Protection
Security is integral to our infrastructure. All telemetry and user logs are sequestered within hardened storage environments, employing advanced encryption protocols to mitigate the risk of unauthorized access or data exfiltration.

### IV. User Privacy Rights
Pursuant to internal governance, members maintain the right to:
1.  **Request Audit:** Inquire regarding the data retained by the SaturnLH infrastructure.
2.  **Request Remediation:** Petition for the modification or deletion of historical interaction data, subject to administrative review to prevent the disruption of critical moderation logs.
